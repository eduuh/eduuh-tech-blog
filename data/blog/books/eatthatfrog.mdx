## Truth about Frogs

If the the first thing you do each morning is to eat a live frog, you can go through the day with the satisfaction of knowing that it is probably the worst thing that is going to happen to you all day long.

Your "frog" is your biggest, most important task, the one you are most likely to procrastinate on if you don't do something about it. It is also the one task that can have the greatest positive impact on your life and result at the moment.

> The first rule of frog eating is this: **If you have to eat two frogs, eat the ugliest one first.**

1. Displine yourself to begin immediately and then to persist until the task is complete before you go on to something else.
2. Resist the temptation to start with the eaesier task.

"The most important decision that i make each day is what i will do immediately and what i will do later if i do it at all"

> The second rule of frog eating is this: **If you have to eat a live frog at all, it doesn't pay to sit and look at it for long.**

*The key to reaching high levels of performance and productivity is to develop the livel on habit of *tackling your major task the first thing each morning*. Develop a routine of "eating your frog" before you do anything else and without taking to much time to think about it*

### Take Action Immediately

Successful, effective people are those who launch directly into their major tasks and then discipline themselves to work steadily and single-mindedly until those tasks are complete.

**Failure to execute** is one of the biggest problem in organizations today. Many people confuse activity with accomplishment. They talk continually, hold endless meetings and worderful plans but in the final analysis, no one does the job and gets the results required.

### Develop the Habits of Success

Your success in life and work will be determined by the kinds of habits that you develop over time.

> The habit of setting priorities, overcoming procrastinations, and getting on with most important task is a mental and physical skill. **This habits are learnable through practise and repetition, over and over again, until it locks into your subconscious mind and become a permanent part of your behaviour.**

Whenever you complete a task of any size or importance, you feel a surge of energy, enthusiasm, and self esteem.The more important the complete task, the happier, more confident and more powerful you feel about yourself and your world.

### Develop a Positive Addiction

When you develop this addiction, you will at an unconscious level, begin to organize your life in such away tht you are continually start and completing ever more important tasks and projects. You will become addicted to sucess and contribution. You will find it easier to complete important tasks than not completing them.

### No Shortcuts.

Practice is the key to mastering any skill. Fortunately, my mind is like a muscle. It grows stronger and more capable with use. With practise, you can learn any behaviour or develop any habit that you consider either desirable or necessary.

### The Three Ds of New Habit Formation

You need three key qualities to develop the habits of focus and concentration

1. Make a **decision** to develop a habit of task completion.
2. **Displine** yourself to practise the principles you are about to learn.
3. Back everything you do with **determination** until the habit is locked in and become a parmanent part of your personality.

### Visualize yourself as You want to Be

A special way that you can accelerate your progress towards becoming highly productiv effective efficient person that you want to be. It consist of your thinking continually about the reward and benefits of being an **action-oriented**, **fast-moving** and **focused person**.

See yourself as the kind of person who gets important jobs done quickly and well on a consistent basis.

Your mental picture of yourself hast a powerfull effect on your behaviour. Visualize yourselve as the person you intend to be in future. **Your self-image** the way you see yourself on the inside, largely determines your performance on the outside.

You have a virtually unlimited ability to learn and develop new skills, habits and abilities. When you train yourself,through repetition and practise, to overcome procastination and get your most important tasks completed quickly, you will move yourself onto the fast tack in you life and career and step on the accelerator.
